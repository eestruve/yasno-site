# Промпты для генерации изображений (Yasno - Nano Banana / Midjourney v6)

**Инструкция:** Используйте эти промпты без изменений. Они оптимизированы для **фотореализма 8K**, **новогодней атмосферы** и **технической точности**.

**Ключевые параметры:**
*   `--style raw` — фотореализм.
*   `--v 6.0` — версия модели.
*   Атмосфера: Fairy tale (сказка), Christmas magic, Cozy (уют).

---

## 1. Главный экран (Hero Section)
**Задача:** Роскошный дом, "зимняя сказка".
**Prompt:**
> architectural photography of a luxury russian country house in Moscow region, mixed brick and wood facade, magical winter night, Christmas fairy tale atmosphere, warm golden lighting on the house and trees, large decorated Christmas tree in the yard service, huge piles of fluffy snow, driveway with luxury cars, soft glow, cozy holiday vibe, shot on Sony A7R IV, 16-35mm wide angle lens, f/8, ISO 100, hyper-realistic, 8k uhd --ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0

> close-up architectural detail shot of LED icicle lights (play light curtains with uneven drops) hanging from a wooden roof eave, the lights are vertical strands of varying lengths creating a jagged fringe effect, warm white light, real frost texture on the wires, heavy snow on the roof overhang, soft bokeh of a winter forest background, magical Christmas atmosphere, sharp focus on the LED bulbs, technical accuracy, 8k --ar 4:3 --style raw --v 6.0
